The Roman Millarium Aureum ( related )
The Distance sign using the Milion. The Roman Millarium Aureum (The Golden Milestone or The Million) was once said to be the milestone from where all Byzantine distances were calculated but on further research this has been changed to the Forum of Constantine in modern Cemberlitas . This important monument is hardly noticeable as it’s a small piece of marble that sits at the beginning of the road that leaves Sultanahmet Square. To its right are remains of an Ottoman water tower and on its left is the 1744 fountain built for Hacı Beşir Ağa who was the Chief Black Eunuch at Topkapi Palace
