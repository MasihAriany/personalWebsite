TEDx ShirazUniversity

TEDx Event Front-End Developer March - September 2016

First TEDx Event in my hometown, Shiraz, needed a very good online impression.
I took the responsibility to develop the Event's Website. Also designing some graphics when needed.
Scroll down to walk through the implementation and design I've done.

The challenge

A website was needed with multiple functionalities for pre-event activities, for during the event and after it.
I also had to take TED Standards and requirments into account. Also I knew that, this Website will have many visitors specially during the registeration period. So there was no place for anything that would made it slow.
As a result, fast responding, easy-to-find of what is important and of course attractiveness was my main goals in this Project.

Forms

During the pre-event phase, fromt time to time the Event needed a form page. For instance, we had a contest for designing a Logo. Or suggesting speakers. Eventually, we needed a form for Registeration.
It was my first time that my code needed to have interactions with back-end and server. At the end, everything worked out well and I learned what is needed in this area.

Other parts

My favorite page in the whole website is "Team". Just the pictures of all crew in a same format, up front, B&W. and made some JS interaction to filter regarding the selected committee from the above menu.

At the end

It was a fun and a little bit challenging project to work on. Challenge part for me was that there was no place for error. This was the first TEDx event in our city and we were in a way, presenting the city internationally.

But there is nothing like a satisfying feedback for the service you've provided.

Unfortunately, our website is not up and running anymore. So I cannot provide a reachable URL for the website.
I try to put the project in my Github as soon as possible.
